About Us

We are TeaRoom. We have been importing, producing and selling tea since 2006.

We bring tea directly from the plantations, we personally know farmers from all over the world. We go to Ukrainian farmers for fragrant herbs and fruits.

We have our own tea production, where we package and beautifully pack tea, make tisanes.

We can easily talk about any of the smallest processes associated with the product. Ask, share!

We improve service, develop production and open new stores. The team and the growth of the company are important for us along with the product.

We at TeaRoom are in love with tea, but we don't serve it. We serve people and everything human. Development, curiosity, openness, beauty - this is the path we are following.

Products

Earl Grey Black Tea

Bold and rich, smooth and sweet; these black teas represent a wide range of flavors. Stash Tea best seller and long-time customer favorite! Earl Grey is black tea scented with bergamot, an oil extracted from a lime-shaped Mediterranean citrus about the size of an orange. Earl Grey brews up a beautiful amber cup with a distinctive flavor and lightly fragrant aroma. Earl Grey is a versatile tea, at its best in the afternoon or after dinner. Delicious and flavorful plain, it may also be served with sugar and milk. Plus, it makes a wonderful, refreshing iced tea.

Premium Green Tea

Stash Tea does crazy bold flavors exceptionally well. Frankly, that’s what we’re known for. But we’re also seasoned pros at creating the best traditional teas. Like this one. You’re not looking at just a green tea. This is Premium Green Tea. We process our Premium Green Tea the same way that Japanese green tea is processed. What’s more, we grow our own green tea in our very own tea garden, just to make sure our quality is truly premium. Arguably, it’s the best green tea. Feel free to @ us, we are pretty confident on this one. All you have to do is look at the bright golden-green color that our green tea brews up. Then you’ll know this green tea is up there with the best.

Oolong Tea

Fragrant magnolia blossoms richly scent this smooth oolong. Its lovely, large leaves are hand-rolled. Jasmine tea lovers will find this tea appealing. Known in China as Lan Hua Oolong, this tea may be infused multiple times to reveal subtle differences. Very pale in the cup. Grown in Fujian Province.

Rooibos Chai Herbal Tea

A delicious herbal chai blend made with South African rooibos and fragrant chai spices. It brews up a full-bodied, aromatic cup that is rich and satisfying. Try it with an added touch of milk and honey.
